Neural Networks Project Proposal

An investigation into the ethics behind Spotify's recommendations

The Spotify algorithm is increasingly depicted as a Leviathan that both depresses and drives our experience and enjoyment of music today. I've started reading about the deep learning mechanisms behind Spotify's recommendations in order to better understand the algorithm behind it and hopefully develop a thorough analysis, critique of, and response to its flaws. The first article I found was written by a former Spotify intern and is provides a detailed report of the work he did with NN, though it was published way back in 2014 and Spotify has amassed much more power and algorithmic capacity since then. I hope to use the tools and theory learned in class to explore in more concrete detail some of the blind spots in their deep learning and propose solutions where I can.

Goals:

  1. Identify and analyze key blind spots to their deep learning algorithms.
  2. Unpack how these blindspots have greater ramifications for the streaming services industry
  3. Propose solutions or alternative approaches.
